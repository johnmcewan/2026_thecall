Est. in KC

More Than a Game

It started with a couch, a TV, and a few good men who refused to watch football alone. Over the years, the Monday Nite Footballers grew from a casual watch party into one of Kansas City's most tightly-knit communities — cutting across neighborhoods, professions, and generations.

From the Crossroads to Westport, Brookside to the Troost Corridor, the MNF crew represents the full breadth of Kansas City life. They are artists, chefs, pastors, data nerds, entrepreneurs — united by the game and connected to this city in ways that run far deeper than any box score.
